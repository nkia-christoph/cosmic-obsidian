# 🪨 cosmic-obsidian

> 🚀 Your [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) vault, right at your fingertips — from the [Pop Launcher](https://github.com/pop-os/launcher).

**cosmic-obsidian** bridges the power of [Pop Launcher](https://github.com/pop-os/launcher) with your [Obsidian.md](https://obsidian.md) knowledge base.

## ✨ What it does

🔍 **Query** — Search your vault without leaving your workflow
📝 **Create** — Spin up new notes in a keystroke
🧭 **Navigate** — Jump straight into any note, folder, or tag

This project is under active development — stay tuned!
